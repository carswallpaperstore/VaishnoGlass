Glass Type Details

Explore our specialized glass and mirror services in depth

Toughened Glass Installation

Our toughened or tempered glass is up to five times stronger than regular glass, making it the ideal choice for safety-critical applications. We specialize in:

  • Shower enclosures and bathroom installations
  • Balcony and terrace railings
  • Entrance doors and sidelights
  • Office partitions and conference room dividers
  • Shopfronts and commercial glazing

All our toughened glass installations come with a 5-year warranty and meet ISI safety standards.

Designer Mirrors

Transform any space with our custom designer mirrors that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal. Our services include:

  • Floor-to-ceiling wall mirrors
  • Decorative frameless mirrors
  • Custom shaped mirrors (round, oval, hexagonal)
  • Back-painted mirrors for feature walls
  • Lighted vanity mirrors for bathrooms

We offer mirrors in various finishes including silver, bronze, gold, and rose gold to match any interior design scheme.

Shower Glass Enclosures

Upgrade your bathroom with our premium frameless shower enclosures that create a spa-like experience. We provide:

  • Frameless glass panels with minimal hardware
  • Sliding and hinged door options
  • Custom sizing to fit any bathroom layout
  • Anti-scale glass treatment for easy maintenance
  • Professional waterproofing and sealing

Our shower enclosures feature 10mm toughened glass with premium German hardware for smooth operation and long-lasting durability.

Glass Doors & Windows

Modern glass doors and windows that enhance natural light while providing energy efficiency. Our offerings include:

  • Sliding glass doors for balconies and terraces
  • Swing and French doors with slim profiles
  • Folding glass doors for open-plan living
  • Double-glazed windows for thermal insulation
  • Custom window grilles and decorative patterns

All our doors and windows feature high-quality hardware systems from Germany and Japan for smooth operation and longevity.

Office Glass Partitions

Create flexible, modern workspaces with our office glass partition solutions. We specialize in:

  • Frameless glass partitions for executive offices
  • Frosted and etched glass for privacy
  • Soundproof glass panels for meeting rooms
  • Interior glazed walls with fire rating options
  • Smart glass solutions with switchable opacity

Our office partitions can be easily reconfigured as your business needs evolve, making them a cost-effective long-term solution.

Custom Glass Work

Bring your unique vision to life with our bespoke glass fabrication services. We create:

  • Custom glass tables and furniture
  • Architectural glass features and installations
  • Glass balustrades and railings
  • Specialized glass for retail displays
  • Artistic glass installations and sculptures

Our skilled craftsmen work with architects, designers, and homeowners to create one-of-a-kind glass solutions that exceed expectations.
